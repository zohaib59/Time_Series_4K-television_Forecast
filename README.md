# Flipkart 4K TV - Predictive Analytics & BI Suite

A high-efficiency, dual-engine intelligence pipeline designed to maximize retail profitability and forecast inventory demand.
This is structured using high-engagement spacing, bold hooks, and zero corporate fluff to maximize reach.
Data without a decision is just noise. 📉
Most retail dashboards look pretty, but they fail to answer the multi-million dollar question:
“If I give a bigger discount today, will it actually drive profitable volume, or just burn my margins?”
To solve this, I built a dual-engine Predictive Analytics & BI Suite tailored for e-commerce intelligence.
Here is how it works under the hood:
⚡ The Predictive Engine: Uses an optimized XGBoost model with automated date and frequency detection. It running full walk-forward time-series validation to forecast a bulletproof 30-day demand horizon.
📊 The Commercial Matrix: Automatically maps out gross vs. net revenue, tracks festival sales spikes, maps consumer demographic shifts, and isolates the exact channel driving the highest conversion rate.
⚠️ The Money Shot: It calculates real-time price elasticity curves and stock-out depletion indices—instantly highlighting the exact tipping point where further discounting destroys profitability.
The best part? It’s completely dynamic. Swap the dataset, change the target variable, and the pipeline adapts instantly.
The bridge between raw rows of data and C-suite strategy just got a lot shorter. 🚀
Check out the breakdown below! 👇

## 🚀 Key Frameworks
* **Part 1: Walk-Forward Time Series Engine** 
  Utilizes **XGBoost** and **Prophet** under rolling time-series validation constraints to forecast 30-day forward horizons with log-stabilized variance.
* **Part 2: Strategic Business Intelligence Matrix**
  A dynamic analytics pipeline mapping brand revenue shares, campaign channel conversions, price elasticity boundaries, and real-time stock-out risks.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
`Python` | `XGBoost` | `Prophet` | `Scikit-Learn` | `Pandas` | `Seaborn`
